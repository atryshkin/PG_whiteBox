LeftMouseClick, RightMouseClick, R -- Attacks
R -- Parry

Y -- swap between two handed and one handed weapon combat style
WORKS ONLY IF WEAPON IS SWITCHABLE
(to make it so, you need to go to the Controller->Inventroy Manager->Cur Weapon->Switchable and 
turn it on)

G -- lock target

Left Shift -- step back
Left Shift(HOLD) -- running
Left Shift + W/A/S/D -- rolling in current direction
